Hash Frontier Software & Mining Support

Hash Frontier's software is free and open-source. That said, by using Hash Frontier's software, you are assuming any and all liability.


You can get direct remote support from Hash Frontier for the following:

1. Hash Kit setup and integration.

2. General Bitcoin mining farm technical consulting.


These services are billed at a rate of $100 an hour, even if the full hour is not utilized. It is recommended that you find ways to fill each hour of support that is contracted. How you do that is up to you.

If you are interested in getting support from Hash Frontier, please send an email about your request to support@hashfrontier.com.

Please put Hash Frontier Support Request in the subject of the email or your request may be automatically filtered out as spam.
